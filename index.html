Activity: "Robothon 2025"

Powered by First EduSpec Inc. and Rex Education, our elementary and high school students completed various robotics challenges. Divided into teams, students not only proved their knowledge and skills in designing, programming and building their prototypes but also displayed teamwork and sportsmanship.

As our student-participants were cheered on by their classmates, parents present at the event were able to have hands-on experience manipulating the prototypes on display. The Robothon 2025 was held last October 21, 2025 at the Sgt. Angel M. Contreras Covered Court.

The highly anticipated Dramafest of the Highschool department was held last October 21, 2025 at the Dionicia M. Contreras Multipurpose Gymnasium. This activity showcased not only the acting prowess of our Bambinians but also highlighted their technical capabilities in staging a one-act play.

The Dramafest brought to life literary pieces that reflect various societal issues still relevant today. Casa 7 and 10 showcased works by Rogelio Sikat; namely "Impeng Negro" and "Tata Selo" respectively. Casa 8 performed a Singaporean literary piece translated by Mauro R. Avena, "Ang Ama". While Casa 9 chose the short story "Sandosenang Sapatos" by Luis Gatmaitan.

Kudos to our High schoolers for conveying the message of these literary pieces through play!
